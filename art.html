Cybernetic Images & Imaginaries

Zine · Performance lecture · 2026

Self-published zine and performance lecture for the New Heretics exhibition at the House of Annetta, London, England (May 2026). The zine investigates a speculative visual archive to think about how the capacious and multivalent ideas of cybernetics and artificial intelligence have found figures and form.

Printmaking

Letterpress · Linocut · Wood carving

“Ceci n’est pas une presse” 2023

Linocut work exploring the contemporary relationship to print technology. Created while serving as Printer’s Apprentice at the Print Room, Massey College, University of Toronto. Prototypes include print-based laptops, keyboards and cell-phones.

“All that is Solid” 2023

Letterpress work displayed at the Talking Walls exhibition, Hart House, Toronto. Explores Marx and Engels’ “all that is solid melts into air” through letterpress.

Photography, Film & Other

Photo · Video · Skateboard

Skateboard Initiative 2019

Queen’s Longboard Club × Step Above Stigma Silent Auction. Community and student artists painted handmade skateboards and longboards; raised $700 ($350 to Step Above Stigma). Skateboards handmade using vacuum-bag air press with sustainably-sourced maple.

07

Doubletake

Double exposure · Film · 2024

An accidental double exposure — an old family camera with a restarted film roll superimposed new photos onto old photos of myself and family over a span of some 18 years. The result is a series exploring memory, loss, and the presence of ghosts: past selves layered into present moments through analog film.
